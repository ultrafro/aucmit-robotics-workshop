Building arms from raw servos instead? Feetech STS3215 bus servos run ~$12.24/pc on AliExpress (12 needed per leader–follower pair, ≈$147 + shipping) — but note the SO-101 leader arm requires specific 7.4 V gear-ratio variants (C044/C046/C001 mix), not 12 identical 12 V units. A LeRobot-specific multi-ratio listing exists at ~$16.46/pc. Verify gear ratios on arrival.

AliExpress search results often show 4-month installment amounts or new-shopper promo prices; all prices above are regular product-page prices.
